About us

London Gophers (https://gophers.london), aka Go London User Group (GLUG), is a London-based community for anyone interested in the Go programming language.

London Gophers provides opportunities to:

- Discuss Go and related topics
- Socialise with friendly people who are interested in Go

We want London Gophers to be a diverse and inclusive community. As such all attendees, organisers and sponsors are required to follow the Go community code of conduct.

Please do not message members without their consent either on this site or on third party platforms such as LinkedIn. If we receive reports of unsolicited messages we will consider it a bannable offense.

Email - contact@gophers.london
Twitter  - https://twitter.com/LondonGophers
YouTube - https://www.youtube.com/c/LondonGophers
GitHub - https://github.com/go-london-user-group

📣 If you'd like to give a talk at a future meetup, don't be shy! We are always looking for new speakers who want to share their adventures with Go and have mentors who can help.

You can sign up to be a speaker here: https://gophers.london/apply/
Do get in touch! We’ll be happy to support you and offer advice if needed 🙂.
