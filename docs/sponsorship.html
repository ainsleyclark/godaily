Why advertise here

A pure-Go, pure-developer audience

No general-interest dilution. Every reader self-selected into a daily Go newsletter — backend, platform, devops and SRE engineers, and the leads who choose their team's tools.

High engagement, by habit

A short, curated, no-noise format readers open every morning. No drip sequences, no upsells, no reader tracking pixels — which is exactly why a clearly-labelled sponsor slot performs.

Trusted editorial

Every story is ranked and summarised for signal across 18 community sources. Your placement borrows that credibility.

Daily cadence, flexible buys

~22 issues a month means you can run a single test or a sustained campaign, and every placement lives on permanently at the web archive.
